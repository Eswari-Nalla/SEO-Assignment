Meta tags can help with search engine optimization (SEO). They can: 
 Help search engines understand the topic of your page
 Help people understand the contents of your page
 Avoid duplicate content issues
 Drive clicks and organic search traffic
Some benefits of using meta tags include: 
 Relevancy: Meta tags show search engines how relevant your page is to the user's query
 Save time: You don't need to write too much
 Optimize your site: Meta tags can optimize your site without making things untidy
 Track metrics: Meta tags allow you to track your metrics better
